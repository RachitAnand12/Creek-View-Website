  • Creek View Pledges To Be "Strong4Life"

    An Atlanta-based hospital's campaign to promote wellness in Georgia has reached Alpharetta's Creek View Elementary School. Children's Healthcare of Atlanta has partnered with the Fulton County School System's Nutrition Department to launch Strong4Life, its movement built to reverse the trend of childhood obesity and associated illnesses in the state. Creek View was one of the schools chosen to participate in Fulton County this year. Children's staff kicked off the program earlier this week at Creek View.

5th Grade Teachers

5th Grade Teachers

The 5th grade team is pleased to welcome all of you to CVES and especially to our classes! We will have a great year of learning new things about the world and ourselves. Starting our year via URL will be more challenging than other years, but working together, we will have a great year. We have a set schedule, and the student will need to be online at certain times and complete some assignments independently. Fifth grade is a big change from the younger grades with new opportunities and more responsibilities such as Student Council, Science Olympiad, CNN, Reading Bowl Team, Chorus, and the Talent Show. As the year gets rolling, some of these activities will hopefully begin. It is our hope you will “dive in” and participate to the best of your abilities and talents both academically and in the extra-curricular activities.
